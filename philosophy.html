Better Yourself,
Better the World.

We at Kalvi, want to nurture the youth and prepare them to solve complex global challenges using technology.

We believe the key lies in helping students become the best versions of themselves and helping them create collaboratively.

The outcomes of every Kalvi program eventually lead to the above. To facilitate goal setting and easy identification of learning gaps, the outcomes are categorised into 3 groups:
Technology Skills

Skills of the core learning area of the program. e.g. Computer Science, Software development

Personal Skills

Learnability, Creativity, Critical thinking etc.

Inter-personal Skills

Communication, Leadership, Collaboration, and General Awareness

To achieve such diverse outcomes, we believe the curriculum needs to be robust yet flexible to change. This is why we have 4 important guidelines that help us build a typical Kalvi curriculum.
1
Be product-first

Help students pick up the required skills to build an end product

2
Use whole-game methodology

Show students a complete working solution and then help them deconstruct it by gradually digging deeper

3
Nurture growth mindset

Help the students learn by themselves and grow to be their best versions

4
Create future leaders

Equip students with all the skills to become great team players and leaders of tomorrow
